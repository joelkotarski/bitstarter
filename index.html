Imagine together we create a platform that allows anyone with a creative drive to bring into reality the one or more things that they wish they could bring to the world, if they were unbound by constraints? This will be thingk.com, the first of many projects that tap into the notion of bringing thingks into the world.



This site, ThingkStarter, has two purposes: To bootstrap funding for thingk.com (the first Thingk we collectively fund), then it will morph into the actual site and split off into a site that will continue to get funding for Thingks that our Creators create.

By funding thingk.com, you will empower many people to create, and by being a founder of the Thingk foundry, we will empower you to create in new and unexpected ways. By investing in our dream, we will invest in yours, pushing to make sure that you have access to the tools to push your Thingk along but also making sure your Thingk comes into reality. Therefore, your project will be become a funded project on ThingkStarter later.
